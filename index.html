A charger-free solution for mobile devices

 

Charge Anywhere

Don't worry about always having a charger with you for your mobile devices or constantly put yourself in places where you have access to an outlet. With Inductive Wireless, your devices can charge anywhere, hassle-free and without any wires.

No Overheating

With Inductive Wireless' state of the art charge threshold filter technology, your device will know when to stop accepting charge in order to always prevent overheating, a problem prevalent in mobile devices today. 

No Hassle

Any mobile device that implements Inductive Wireless comes with a charging pack which plugs into any  AC power outlet. When the charge pack is plugged in, it is constantly listening  to be told to start sending a charge signal to the device. 

Self-Policing

When your phone needs to be charged, it sends a signal to the charge pack to start charging using a wireless connection. When the phone has been sufficiently charged it automatically turns off the charging mechanism.
